Photography

To me, photography is about documenting everyday life and society on the streets. In essence, the reason I've always been drawn to photography is because of its nature to be dependent on chance. Through photography I am able to capture life's moments as they swirl around me.

“Above all, I craved to seize the whole essence, in the confines of one single photograph, of some situation that was in the process of unrolling itself before my eyes.”
— Henri Cartier-Bresson
